The Nerds in Our Herd

To say our nerds are highly qualified would be an understatement. From prodigies who taught themselves to program as children, to bona fide Mac geniuses, to skilled teachers, their sheer brainpower keeps the ranch going—and growing. Our nerds have authored books, created apps, sold companies, co-founded international programmer groups, and even won a Grammy. But the coolest thing is, they’re also some of the nicest people you may ever meet.
